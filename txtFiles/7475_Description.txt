<p>Nevada Humane Society is a non-profit, charitable, no-kill&nbsp;organization creating and sustaining a no-kill community in Northern Nevada. Their mission is to promote animal welfare and to provide for the relief and prevention of all animal suffering in Northern Nevada. Nevada Humane Society recently partnered with Carson City Animal Services&nbsp;and now has two locations, one in Reno and one in Carson City. Their work is made possible by donations and the generous community that surrounds them.</p>
