<p>Animal Care Sanctuary is a no-kill rescue/sanctuary on 128 beautiful acres in rural North East Pennsylvania.&nbsp; </p>
<p>Our mission is to provide a safe haven for displaced, abused or abandoned animals until they transition into warm, loving homes.</p>
<p>We strive to be a leader in animal rescue by providing optimal care for animals, while providing education and resources for those who look after them.&nbsp; </p>
