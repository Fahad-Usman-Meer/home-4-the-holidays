Welcome to Petite Paws Rescue, Inc.&nbsp; We are an approved&nbsp; 501(C)(3) charity organization, focusing on the rescue of the Yorkshire Terrier, but no paw too small.&nbsp; <br />
<br />
Our home base is located in Indiana with offices in Ohio also.&nbsp;&nbsp; We do not have a shelter facility.&nbsp; All of our available dogs are located in private foster homes.&nbsp; Our volunteers and members are located within Indiana (NW IN, NE IN &amp; Indianapolis area) NE Illinois and NW Ohio.<br />
<br />
We are in NEED of Volunteers and Foster Homes in Indiana and within 60 miles of Indiana's borders in our sister-states of Ohio, Illinois, Kentucky and Michigan.<br />
