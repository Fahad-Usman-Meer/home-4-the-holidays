Forgotten Cats is a non-profit, all volunteer cat rescue located in Greenville, DE, and serving the tri-state area.&nbsp; Our mission is to humanely reduce the homeless and unwanted cat population through Trap/Neuter/Release (TNR).&nbsp; Forgotten Cats finds homes for socialized cats through three PetSmart Adoption Centers in DE and PA.&nbsp; We sterilize and average of 350-400 cats and adopt out 70-80 socialized cats each month.&nbsp;
