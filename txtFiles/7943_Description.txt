<h3>Millington Animal Safe Haven&nbsp;Canine Rescue- M.A.S.H.</h3>

<p>Millington Animal Safe Haven is a group of volunteers in Millington, Tennessee and the surrounding areas who are devoted to loving and saving dogs! We foster dogs in our homes and provide them with the care, training and love needed to become great family members.&nbsp;Dogs are special beings that God placed here to be &quot;man&#39;s best friend&quot;. They give unconditional love to humans all over the world! We welcome those that want to participate in our mission to love, save, heal and find a &quot;forever home&quot; for each of our rescued pups. Please enjoy the site and find your special friend!</p>
