The Lawrence County Humane Society Animal Shelter, established in 1995,&nbsp;is owned and operated by the Lawrence County Humane Society and&nbsp;currently serves the communities of Lawrence County and Magoffin County, KY.&nbsp; We are a 501(c)3 organization in Louisa, KY,&nbsp;devoted to the rescue, adoption and well-being of homeless, abused and neglected animals, cruelty&nbsp;prevention&nbsp;and&nbsp;humane education.&nbsp; We are also mandatory spay/neuter and no animal leaves our care until he or she is spayed/neutered.&nbsp;
