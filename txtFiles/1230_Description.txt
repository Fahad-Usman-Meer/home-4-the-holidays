<p class="MsoNormal" style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt" align="center"><strong><span style="FONT-FAMILY: Arial"><font size="3">PICKET FENCE POODLE RESCUE MISSION STATEMENT</font></span></strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"><strong><span style="FONT-FAMILY: Arial"><font size="3"></font></span></strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"><strong><span style="FONT-FAMILY: Arial"><font size="3">PICKET FENCE POODLE RESCUE</font></span></strong><span style="FONT-FAMILY: Arial"><font size="3"> is a charitable and educational non-profit 501(c)(3) organization to rescue and supply Poodles with love, shelter, food, human companionship, medical care and spay-neuter services until permanent placement is secured into a pre-screened loving home, as long as the Poodle is not dangerous to humans or other dogs.</font></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"><span style="FONT-FAMILY: Arial"><font size="3"><o:p></o:p></font></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"><font size="3"><strong style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal"><span style="FONT-FAMILY: Arial">PICKET FENCE POODLE RESCUE</span></strong><span style="FONT-FAMILY: Arial"> will accept an adoptable Poodle who is either abandoned in a shelter, or purchased/rescued by any concerned person, or living with an owner who can no longer care for the Poodle.&nbsp; We also participate in rescue and placement of Poodles who are culled from Breeders or Puppy Mills.</span></font></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"><font size="3"><span style="FONT-FAMILY: Arial"></span></font></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"><font size="3"><strong style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal"><span style="FONT-FAMILY: Arial">PICKET FENCE POODLE RESCUE</span></strong><span style="FONT-FAMILY: Arial"> feels educating and informing the public about the commercial dog industry in <st1:country-region w:st="on"><st1:place w:st="on">America</st1:place></st1:country-region> and about responsible pet ownership is very important as well as reviving the spirit of volunteerism in our great county.</span></font></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"><font size="3"><span style="FONT-FAMILY: Arial"></span></font></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"><font size="3"><strong style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal"><span style="FONT-FAMILY: Arial">PICKET FENCE POODLE RESCUE</span></strong><span style="FONT-FAMILY: Arial"> wishes to eliminate the euthanasia of any healthy, adoptable Poodles.&nbsp; We do not accept euthanasia as a means of population control for animals.&nbsp; We believe each Poodle's life is meaningful and each deserves a loving home.&nbsp; We take responsibility for each Poodle rescued and placed through Picket Fence Poodle Rescue for the rest of the dogs' natural life.<o:p></o:p></span></font></p>
