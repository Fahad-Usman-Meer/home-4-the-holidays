<div style="margin: 5pt 0.5in; text-align: justify;"><span style="font-size: 13pt;">Homes for Orphaned Pets Exist (HOPE) is an IRS designated 501(c)3 non-profit animal rescue run by volunteers who receive no compensation.&nbsp;</span></div>
<div style="margin: 5pt 0.5in; text-align: justify;"><span style="font-size: 13pt;">HOPE provides foster care and finds homes for animals that have been surrendered by their owner, picked up as strays, abandoned, or removed from their home due to neglect or abuse.&nbsp; </span></div>
<div style="margin: 5pt 0.5in; text-align: justify;"><span style="font-size: 13pt;">In 2011 HOPE proudly opened&nbsp;a low-cost spay and neuter clinic for&nbsp;low-income owners of cats and dogs.&nbsp; The clinic is located on Route 50 in Wilton, NY.&nbsp;</span></div>
<div style="margin: 5pt 0.5in; text-align: justify;"><span style="font-size: 13pt;"></span></div>
<div style="margin: 5pt 0.5in; text-align: justify;"><span style="font-size: 13pt;">Other ways we&nbsp;help:</span></div>
<div style="margin: 5pt 0.5in; text-align: justify;"><span style="font-size: 13pt;"></span><span style="font-size: 13pt;">- assisting local families who are unable to afford veterinary care for pets in need of urgent or critical medical treatment</span></div>
<div style="margin: 5pt 0.5in; text-align: justify;"><span style="font-size: 13pt;"></span><span style="font-size: 13pt;">- providing pet food to local food pantries for distribution</span><span style="font-size: 13pt;">.</span></div>
