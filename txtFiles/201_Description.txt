<p>Bryant Animal Shelter serves as the animal shelter and offices of Bryant Animal Control. &nbsp;We serve a community of approximately 20,000 residents. &nbsp;We conduct adoptions 6 days a week, and at our closest PetSmart Store on most Sundays.</p>

<p>The average cost to adopt a pet is only $95.00 and it includes spay/neuter, vaccinations, testing for Heartworm or FeLV/FIV (when age-appropriat,) de-worming and flea/tick preventative for the first 30 days. &nbsp;</p>
