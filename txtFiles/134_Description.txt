<a href="http://www.buffalohumane.org/" target="_blank">Buffalo Humane</a> is a New York State incorporated nonprofit organization and an <a href="http://www.buffalohumane.org/library/BH_501c3_letter_only.pdf" target="_blank">IRS tax-exempt 501(c)3 public charity</a> devoted to animal welfare in the region. Our mission is to promote and protect the health, safety, and welfare of pets and people by taking in and placing homeless companion animals, encouraging and supporting spay/neuter, providing community outreach to ensure proper animal stewardship, and promoting rational and effective animal care and control policies at all levels of government. We operate on a 100% volunteer basis, and our activities are funded exclusively through fundraising, contributions, and donations. <a target="_blank" href="http://www.buffalohumane.org/donate.html">Your help is greatly needed</a> so that we can continue our work on behalf of area animals.</p>
