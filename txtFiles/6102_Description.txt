<h3><font color="Green"><font color="red"><font color="Green"><font color="red"><font color="Green"><font color="red"><font color="Green"><font color="black">Who We Are:</font></font></font></font></font></font></font></font></h3>
<font color="Green"><font color="red"><font color="Green"><font color="red"><font color="Green"><font color="red"><font color="Green"><font color="black"><br />
<br />
Mist`nStone Labrador Retriever Rescue assistance group, is located SW Pa. We are mostly made up of a few friends and family members who volunteer their time to help, but because we have no physical shelter, we are unable to house Labradors in need, we work in the best way that we can. This means until we have a foster ready, we can't save a Lab from a kill shelter situation! <br />
<br />
<em><strong>We can't pull a Lab until we have a foster home, so if you're in SW Pa and interested in becoming a foster parent, please let us know! </strong></em> 		 		<br />
<br />
We try our best to save unwanted labs in pounds, kill shelters and from individuals who request we &quot;take&quot; or help them place their lab. We work at getting them into foster homes, and preparing them for a loving forever home. <br />
<br />
While our main focus is saving the Labrador Retriever currently sitting on death row in kill shelters across the United States, we would not be able to do this without people who want to adopt them! <br />
<br />
Although we work mostly with purebred Labradors, if a Labrador mix shows he has that great Labrador attitude, and characteristics, we aren't beyond helping mixes! Some of the best dogs are mixed! <br />
<br />
We seek out and welcome families and individuals looking for a Labrador, and do what we can to ensure you find a great match that will happily fit into your life! Most of our matches are pulled directly from high kill shelters, or people giving up their lab, however many of the Labradors in our program will have spent time with one of Mist`nStone&rsquo;s foster families as well! <br />

    <font color="Green"><font color="red"><font color="Green"><font color="red"><font color="Green"><font color="red"><font color="Green"><font color="black">		 We prefer that all of our Labs will have spent time in a foster home to assess temperament and to ensure their health. There are rare exceptions to this, and we would inform you before hand of any dogs special situation if applicable. <br />
    All of our Labs will be spayed or neutered before adoption. The only exception would be young puppies and they will go with a spay/neuter contract. <br />
    <br />
    We do consider the first 2 weeks of any adoption as a &quot;trial&quot; period. After this time is up, if you and the dog are still happy, we consider the adoption a success. This period allows you to have your new best friend vet checked for any health issues, and to see if this is the Lab you are really looking for, and if you are the best family for the Labrador!. <br />
