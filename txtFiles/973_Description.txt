&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; The Caruthersville Animal Shelter cooperates with the CIty of Caruthersville Animal Control.&nbsp; We handle about 2500 animals a year through the Shelter.&nbsp; We work with other city's animal control officers in the county for adoptable animals.&nbsp; The investigators at the Caruthersville Humane Society work with law enforcement officials to investigate cruelty and neglect cases. Located at 500 East Industrial Drive, the Shelter is open Monday through Friday 11-5 and every other Saturday 10-2.&nbsp; Shelter Manager is Erma Page with Humane Society employee Matt Duckworth.&nbsp; Karol Wilcox is President of the Humane Society and Chief Investigator.
