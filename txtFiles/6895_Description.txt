<font size="3" face="Book Antiqua"><span style="LINE-HEIGHT: 20px; FONT-SIZE: 16px">As with most Animal Rescue groups, STARS is represented by an all volunteer Board of Directors.<br />
We all have full time jobs and families to care for. That does not stop us from finding some time to spend trying to save as many pets as possible!<br />
We are a very small group of foster homes, specializing in all-breed dog rescue.<br />
We will pull sick animals and animals facing euthanasia from animal control facilities all over the state if we have funds for care and space to foster.<br />
Most of these animals have been rescued from wandering local roads or were picked up at locations where they were abandoned.<br />
<br />
We will also take owner surrenders if foster homes available.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<br />
