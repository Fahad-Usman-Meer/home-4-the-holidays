<p align="center"><font face="arial"><strong><font size="4"><st1:place>MISSION</st1:place> STATEMENT </font></strong></font></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: center; mso-outline-level: 1"><strong><font face="arial" size="4"></font></strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: center; mso-outline-level: 1" align="center"><font face="arial" size="3">Our purpose for founding Sunny Meadows...Safe Haven for Pets:</font></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"><o:p><font face="arial" size="3">&nbsp;</font></o:p></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"><font face="arial" size="3">In a world of staggering, heart breaking numbers of pets euthanized each year, to offer safety from the cruel reality for as many pets as possible.</font></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"><o:p><font face="arial" size="3">&nbsp;</font></o:p></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; mso-outline-level: 1"><font face="arial" size="3">Enhance, nurture and extend the value of life in stray, injured, unwanted and abused pets.</font></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"><o:p><font face="arial" size="3">&nbsp;</font></o:p></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"><font face="arial" size="3">Dedicated to successfully placing our rescued pets in conscientious, compassionate and everlasting, loving homes.</font></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"><o:p><font face="arial" size="3">&nbsp;</font></o:p></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"><font face="arial" size="3">Reduce unwanted litters by educating, influencing, promoting and organizing sterilization programs.</font></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"><o:p><font face="arial" size="3">&nbsp;</font></o:p></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"><font face="arial" size="3">Reduce relinquishment of companion animals by promoting preventative medical care, promoting compatibility in the selection of a pet, to educate responsible pet ownership, to offer resources and advice on behavioral issues, and ultimately promoting the benefits of having a cherished, loving pet.</font></p>
