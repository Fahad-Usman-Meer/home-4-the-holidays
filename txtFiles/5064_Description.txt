<div style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: normal" align="center">Mission Statement</div>
<div style="MARGIN: auto auto 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: normal">&nbsp;</div>
<div style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: normal">The mission of Biker Bill&rsquo;s Tigger Land Cat Sanctuary is to provide safe haven to feral cats of southern Brevard County (Florida) that have lost their homes (i.e., feral colony) through man-made or natural disaster, have been found living in an unsafe area (e.g., by a busy roadway) or in situations that require their relocation.&nbsp;The Sanctuary will be established to provide clean, comfortable living conditions with ample food and water and necessary medical care to include annual check-ups and vaccinations. </div>
<div style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: normal">&nbsp;</div>
<span style="FONT-SIZE: 11pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 115%">The secondary, and necessary, mission of BTLCS is the fostering and adoption of feral cats that are found to be social and suitable for adoption.&nbsp;This includes kittens born of feral cats.&nbsp;These kittens will be tested for Feline AIDS and Leukemia, spayed or neutered, receive age-appropriate vaccinations, be micro-chipped and treated regularly for internal and external parasites.</span>
