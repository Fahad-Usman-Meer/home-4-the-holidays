<p>Home At Last Animal Haven (HALAH) is an animal rescue organization striving to provide a second chance at life for friendless animals.&nbsp;<br />
Home At Last is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization and receives no government funding, depending entirely on kind donations from individuals or businesses to care for our animals. HALAH cares for friendless dogs in a no-kill environment until they are adopted into a permanent loving home.&nbsp;<br />
