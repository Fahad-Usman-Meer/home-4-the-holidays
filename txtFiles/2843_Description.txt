<h3 align="center"><font color="#008080">Our Mission:</font></h3>
<p align="center">The Burlington County Animal Shelter is a county facility supported by animal control contracts with local municipalities and the county taxpayers.</p>
<p align="center">It is used as a shelter for stray dogs, cats and other small animals for a short term stay in accordance with Title 8, Chapter 23.</p>
<p align="center">All stray animals not claimed by owners or adopted will be kept for at least the state-mandated 7 days. </p>
