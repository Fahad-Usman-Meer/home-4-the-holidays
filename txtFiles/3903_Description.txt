<p>&nbsp; The Northumberland Humane Society is a charitable community resource, dedicated to protecting and providing tempory shelter and comfort to all animals in need and promoting adoption and rehoming for abandoned and unwanted companion animals.&nbsp; We promote humane treatment of all living things and work within the law to respond and investigate all complaints of cruelty and neglect.&nbsp; </p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp; The Northumberland Humane Society endeavours to eliminate cruelty and over-population and we support the principle that no person has the right to cause pain and suffering to any living creature.</p>
