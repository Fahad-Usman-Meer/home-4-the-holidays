<p align="center"><font size="4">Healing Hearts Small Animal Rescue, Inc.&nbsp;is a 501 (c) (3) non profit organization. We are funded solely through donations and fund raisers.&nbsp; </font><font size="4">Our mission is to improve the lives of homeless, abandoned, neglected and abused animals through rescue and rehabilitation. </font></p>
<p align="center"><font size="4">We specialize in the rescue of small mammals <em>(rats, guinea pigs, hamsters, etc)</em>, birds, reptiles, small exotics <em>(sugar gliders, chinchillas, etc)</em> and dogs under 25 lbs.</font></p>
<p align="center"><font size="4">Members of our staff are licensed wildlife rehabilitators by the State of North Carolina. We rehabilitate gray squirrels, opposums, groundhogs, turtles, rabbits and other small mammals. It is imperative that you please contact us as soon as possible when you find orphaned or injured wildlife. </font></p>
