<p style="TEXT-ALIGN: left" align="left"><font style="LINE-HEIGHT: normal" face="arial,helvetica,sans-serif" color="#000000" size="3">B.A.R.K. is a canine rescue group started in April of 2009. B.A.R.K. is completely volunteer based, and made up of people who have a combined 40 years of experience working with all types of rescued animals. </font></p>
<p style="TEXT-ALIGN: left" align="left"><font style="LINE-HEIGHT: normal" face="arial,helvetica,sans-serif" color="#000000" size="3">Being Advocates for Rescued K-9's is a goal we work towards with each and every dog we save. We&nbsp;bring dogs into foster homes, saving one life at a time, and educate the public on the importance of rescue. </font></p>
<p style="LINE-HEIGHT: normal; TEXT-ALIGN: left" align="left"><font style="LINE-HEIGHT: normal" face="arial,helvetica,sans-serif" color="#000000" size="3">Our foster homes&nbsp;take great pride in working with each dog's personality and unique behaviors to find the perfect &quot;forever home&quot; for each dog. </font></p>
