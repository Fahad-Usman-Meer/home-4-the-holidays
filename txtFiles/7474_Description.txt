FosterCat, Inc. is a 501 c 3 all volunteer organization located in Pittsburgh, PA.&nbsp; FosterCat, Inc. is a network of foster homes and volunteers whose motto is, &quot;saving lives, one cat at a time.&quot;&nbsp; We provide temporary, loving caregiving at foster homes and then showcase our cats for adoption at the local Petsmart stores.&nbsp; Since our&nbsp;beginnings in 1999, we have successfully placed over 1,050 cats in loving, permanent homes.
