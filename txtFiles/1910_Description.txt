The Pet Connection is a cageless, no kill facility who strives to take care of their pets in a setting as close to home as possible. We provide premiere care to the pets in our facility, modeling appropriate pet owning behaviors for our community. We provide proper nutrition, humane rehabilitative care, training, companionship and exercise to all in our care. We also operate a for public veterinarian clinic/spay/neuter service for low income persons and a behavioral and obedience program for those needing assistance with common pet problems. Please support our philosophy and efforts by adopting from us. Over 70% of our dogs come from shelters where they were scheduled to be euthanized....
