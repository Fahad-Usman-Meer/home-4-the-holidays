<p>Tiger&#39;s Den Animal Rescue is a Rhode Island-based, non-profit, 501c3, no-kill, 100% volunteer organization which assists families and their pets with fundraising for veterinary bills.</p>

<p>In addition to our family-based program, we maintain a free-roaming and stray cat colony in Rhode Island.&nbsp; We properly vet all of the cats and place the stray cats in loving forever homes.</p>
