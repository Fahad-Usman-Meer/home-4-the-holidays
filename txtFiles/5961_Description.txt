<strong><blockquote><font face="Tahoma" size="2"><strong>Who are we?</strong></font><font face="Tahoma"> </font>
<p style="MARGIN-TOP: 0px; MARGIN-BOTTOM: 0px"><font face="Tahoma" size="2">Our rescue is active in Maryland, Northern Virginia, D.C.,&nbsp; and south central Pennsylvania.&nbsp; We are a group of volunteers, dedicated to rescuing and placing German Shepherds in good homes.</font></p>
<strong>
<p style="TEXT-ALIGN: center" align="center"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Tahoma">Our Mission Statement: </span><font face="Tahoma" size="2">MAGSR is dedicated to rehabilitating and providing a second chance to German shepherds and shepherd mixes that have been abandoned, abused, lost, strayed, or surrendered. Through our community outreach events around the Mid-Atlantic region, we teach and encourage responsible pet ownership and pet population control. Our all-volunteer organization works diligently to locate loving and responsible owners who wish to make their new dog(s) members of their family -- forever. MAGSR remains committed to helping end the needless overpopulation, death, abuse, and neglect of dogs in our area and in improving the lives of the dogs as well as their new owners</font></p>
