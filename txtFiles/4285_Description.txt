<p class="MsoNormal" style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 12pt -12pt"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: Verdana">Safe Cat Foundation, a California based non-profit 501 (c) 3 corporation, was formed to address the safety issues of domestic cats in our local animal shelters and in our society. <o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 12pt -12pt"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: Verdana">We provide education and advocacy for various health and safety issues that affect cats in the home, in the wild or in shelters. <o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 12pt -12pt"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: Verdana">Safe Cat Foundation also provides rescue for cats abandoned at county animal shelters through fostering, providing medical care, spay and neuter services and adoption programs.</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; COLOR: black"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
