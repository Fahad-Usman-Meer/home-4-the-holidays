<br />
This mission will be accomplished with <span class="RedBold9"><nobr><a class="FAtxtL" id="FALINK_2_0_1" href="http://www.fochp.org/_about.asp#">training</a></nobr>,								veterinary services, spay &amp; neuter assistance and rescue</span>.
