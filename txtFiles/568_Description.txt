<p>Friends of Cats, Inc is a true no-kill cat shelter located in El Cajon, Ca. We are open from Tuesday thru Sunday from 10am to 4pm. Kittens are $100, adult cats under 10 years old are $75, and cats 10 and over are $35. All cats are FelV and FIV tested negative, current on FVRCP vaccinations, spayed/neutered, microchipped, and come with a free vet visit within 10 days of adoption.</p>
