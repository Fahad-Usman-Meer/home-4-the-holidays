<strong>Sweet Binks is a 501c3 nonprofit, all volunteer shelter and educational organization. Here you will find adoptable rabbits looking for loving, permanent homes. Sweet Binks is a state licensed shelter and not a foster home network. All adoptable, rescued rabbits are located at our shelter facility which is located in Foster, Rhode Island. Sweet Binks is dedicated to helping homeless, abandoned and neglected rabbits and educating to prevent abandonment and improve the lives of companion rabbits. Sweet Binks assists area shelters with 150 to 200 abandoned rabbits a year. We hope you will considering adopting from Sweet Binks Rabbit Rescue!</strong>
