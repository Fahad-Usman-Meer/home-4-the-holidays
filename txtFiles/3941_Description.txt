<p>Welcome to Pickering Animal Services.&nbsp; In&nbsp;2013 we have housed over 400 animals and adopted out 255 into new homes. We still have many animals that are eagerly waiting to meet their new families. Visit our website at pickering.ca/animals and meet your next family member.</p>

<p>Our shelter is open 6 days a week and is located at 1688 Highway 7 in North Pickering. Call us at 905.427.0093 or visit our website at pickering.ca/animals for more information.</p>

<p>Our adoption fees are $100 for cats/kittens and $200 for dogs.&nbsp; All animals are sterilized, vaccinated, dewormed and mirochipped.</p>
