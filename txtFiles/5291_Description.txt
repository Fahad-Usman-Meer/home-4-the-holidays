<strong>LRRoC </strong>takes in Labrador Retrievers, Golden Retrievers, Newfoundlands and occasional other breeds to rehome.&nbsp; Our dogs come from owner relinquish for a variety of reasons, shelters, strays and special needs from veterinarians.&nbsp; We are a 501(c).3 non profit, all-volunteer organization.&nbsp; 100% of our donations are used for our dogs.&nbsp; Visit our website at <a href="http://www.rescuealab.com">www.rescuealab.com</a>.&nbsp;
