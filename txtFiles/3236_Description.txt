<p><font size="4">Yadkin County Humane Society is a 100% volunteer based non-profit organization.&nbsp; Our mission is to improve the lives of animals, with a focus on Yadkin County, North Carolina, through our foster care/adoption, low and no-cost spay/neuter, and humane education programs.&nbsp; We</font><font size="4"> do not have a shelter building, all animals are cared for in member's homes until they are adopted. In 2006, our seven foster homes adopted 58 kittens/cats, 72 puppies/dogs, and 1 ferret.&nbsp;&nbsp;</font>&nbsp;</p>
