The Animal welfare Society of Jefferson County, WV is a non-profit 501(c3) organization, which operates a no-kill shelter for cats, kittens, dogs and puppies, a spay/neuter program and humane education programs.&nbsp; The animals that are adopted from the shelter are fully vaccinated, tested for diseases and spayed or neutered prior to adoption.&nbsp; The shelter houses an average of 100 animals and also occupies 6 cat cages at the local Petco in Ranson, WV.&nbsp; The society participates in Petco adoption days every other Saturday.&nbsp; Upcoming events and pets that are available for adoption can be viewed on the website at <a href="http://www.awsjc.org">www.awsjc.org</a>.
