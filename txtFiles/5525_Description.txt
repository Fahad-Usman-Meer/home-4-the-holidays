Animal Rescue Foundation is a non-profit 501(c)3 charity dedicated to reducing euthanasia of adoptable pets through foster care and shelter adoption programs, helping to bring a quality life to all abandoned, abused and neglected animals in Tucson. ARF provides support to the community through our hotline and adoption events, providing information and referrals on the following: <br />
<br />
<ul>
    <li>Adopting a pet</li>
    <li>Spay/neuter&nbsp;</li>
    <li>Vaccinations and animal care&nbsp;</li>
    <li>Training and community resources <br />
    </li>
</ul>
<br />
ARF works in collaboration with Pima Animal Care Center to facilitate adoptions of homeless and abandoned animals. Through this partnership, ARF:<br />
<br />
<ul>
    <li>Provides adoption services at PACC on weekdays and on Saturdays and Sundays maintaining adoption hours when the facility would otherwise be closed.</li>
    <li>Organizes and conducts off site adoption days for PACC animals.&nbsp;</li>
    <li>Rescues animals at risk of euthanasia because of overcrowding and places them into volunteer foster homes until permanent homes can be found. <br />
    </li>
</ul>
