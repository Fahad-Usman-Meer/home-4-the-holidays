<p><a name="Mission"><strong>Mission</strong></a></p>

<p><a name="Mission"> </a></p>

<p>Lake Haven is an all-volunteer private, non-profit animal rescue shelter dedicated to the care and adoption of homeless and/or injured dogs, cats, kittens and puppies in the West Michigan area.</p>

<p><a name="Services0"><strong>Goals</strong></a></p>

<p><a name="Mission20"> (1) Provide temporary shelter for homeless cats and dogs;</a><br />
(2) Ensure that at the time of entry to Lake Haven, animals receive needed medical care;<br />
(3) Provide spay/neuter services via area veterinarians;<br />
(4) Place animals in responsible and loving adoptive homes; and<br />
(5) Educate pet owners and the public in proper animal care and the urgent need to spay and neuter their pets.</p>
