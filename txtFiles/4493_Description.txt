<font style="COLOR: blue">AURORAS</font> (Animals United Rescue Organization for Rehabilitation and Adoption Services) is a 501(c)(3), Non-Profit, No-Kill animal rescue organization. We are dedicated to the principle of rescuing stray, unwanted, abused, and lost animals (cats and dogs) for the purpose of rehabilitation and adoption efforts. In addition. Spaying and neutering is essential in order to reduce the growing number of rescue animals each year. <font style="COLOR: blue">AURORAS</font> is an all volunteer organization and has no paid employees, using foster homes til adoption. &nbsp;As the name of the organization suggests, we are committed to the <em><strong><font style="COLOR: green">Dawn of a New Beginning for All Animals.</font></strong></em><br />
