Animal Resource Foundation (ARF) is dedicated to saving and improving the lives of campanion animals.&nbsp; Since its founding in 1999 ARF has helped thousands of cats and dogs from the danger of euthanasia.&nbsp; Special medical services are provided for disabled and abused animals.&nbsp; Each animal receives routine vaccinations, are spayed and neutered prior to adopton and specal needs animals are rehabilitated.&nbsp; Every animal that enters ARF is loved and cared for as if they were our own before they are adopted to carefully selected loving homes.
