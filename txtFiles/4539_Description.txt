<p>The Ward-Wiseman Animal Haven is a no-kill facility in Collinsville, OK (just north of Tulsa).&nbsp; We opened our doors in January, 2008 and have already adopted out almost 200 pets to new homes!&nbsp; WWAH is run on 100% donations and was built from the ground up by volunteers.&nbsp; We are on Petfinder.com or you can visit our website at wwahonline.org.&nbsp; </p>
