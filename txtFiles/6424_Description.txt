We are the largest rescue of its kind in the midwest, taking in only purebred cats from 4 states. We average 700 adoptions a year from our foster-based program. This is a high effective, highly respected organization which is 501c3 and recognized by most of the major shelters in the area. For more information please see our website at <a href="http://www.purebredcatrescue.org">www.purebredcatrescue.org</a>
