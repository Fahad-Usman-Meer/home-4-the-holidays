<p>Catman2 is the largest cats-only cage-free no-kill shelter, adoption center and sanctuary in Western North Carolina.&nbsp; It is a unique shelter that is a home-like environment and has only one employee and a handful of volunteers to care for nearly 90 cats at any given time.&nbsp; Catman2 was founded by retired biology professor Dr. Harold Sims and has been in operation since 1996.</p>
