<p>Tails of Aloha is the first shelter in Hawaii&nbsp;specifically for owners who enter hospice, a nursing home, foreclosure, divorce, or who are elderly and can no longer care for their pets.</p>
<p>Our program also has an animal assisted therapy program, reading program at schools, participates in the feral spay/neuter and microchipping of cats, works with autistic children, assists with training dogs to become service dogs, and an animal rescue program.</p>
<p>Donations are always needed and&nbsp;welcome!</p>
