<p><strong>Our Mission:</strong><br />
We promote animal and human welfare through:</p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<ul>
	<li>care and adoption of homeless animals;</li>
	<li>programs that enrich people&#39;s lives through animal interaction.</li>
</ul>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p>Our Methods:</p>

<p>We seek to fulfill our Mission by providing:</p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<ul>
	<li>an open-admission shelter where homeless animals receive compassionate treatment, medical care, and adoption opportunities;</li>
	<li>life-saving approaches to animal care&nbsp;including animal fostering, transportation, and off site adoptions;</li>
	<li>community programs that unite people and animals for learning opportunities and enjoyment; and</li>
	<li>educational programs that reduce animal abuse, pet overpopulation, and euthanasia.</li>
</ul>
