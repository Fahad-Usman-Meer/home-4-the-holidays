<p align="left"><font color="#000080">Our Mission </font></p>
<p style="LINE-HEIGHT: 200%; MARGIN: 0in 27pt 0pt" class="MsoNormal"><font size="3"><font color="#000000"><font face="Times New Roman">Coastal Bend Small Breed Rescue is a non-profit no-kill humane animal rescue organization dedicated to providing a second chance in a loving home for small companion animals. We promote increased adoptions, spaying and neutering of pets and community involvement to spread the word about the needs of small breed &ldquo;fur-kids&rdquo;.<span style="mso-bidi-font-weight: bold"> We serve the community by utilizing caring people who are dedicated to rescuing and saving companion animals.<o:p></o:p></span></font></font></font></p>
