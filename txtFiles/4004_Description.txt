<p>We are a non-profit, 501(c)3 animal rescue organization who saves dogs, puppies, cats and kittens from kill shelters.&nbsp; ALL of our pets live in foster homes.</p>
<p>Before a pet is adopted, they are:</p>
<p>*Neutered/Spayed<br />
*Medically examined by a licensed veterinarian<br />
*Is current on vaccinations<br />
*Heart Worm tested </p>
<p>*Routinely treated monthly with a Heart Worm preventative (if old enough)<br />
*Routinely treated monthly with a flea/tick preventative (if old enough)<br />
*Professionally groomed</p>
