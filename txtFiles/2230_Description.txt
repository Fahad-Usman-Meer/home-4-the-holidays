<div style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt"><font size="3">Purpose of No More Homeless Pets KC:<br />
</font></span></div>
<div style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt">No More Homeless Pets KC is a non-profit animal welfare organization.&nbsp;Our vision is a lifelong, caring home for every pet in KC.&nbsp;Sadly, each year approximately 20,000 animals are killed in our area shelters.&nbsp;We believe the solution is:</span></div>
<span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt"><span>
<ol style="MARGIN-TOP: 0in" type="1">
    <li style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; COLOR: black; LINE-HEIGHT: 12pt"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt">Creating awareness of the issue</span> </li>
    <li style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; COLOR: black; LINE-HEIGHT: 12pt"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt">High volume, low/no cost spay/neuter</span> </li>
    <li style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; COLOR: black; LINE-HEIGHT: 12pt"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt">TNR (Trap/Neuter/Return) for Feral and Free Roaming cats</span> </li>
    <li style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; COLOR: black; LINE-HEIGHT: 12pt"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt">Promoting adoption from local shelters and rescue groups</span> </li>
</ol>
<div style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt">To achieve our goal of a No Kill KC by 2012, we are building a cooperative effort across the KC animal welfare community</span>.</div>
<div style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"></div>
<div style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"></div>
<font color="#000000"><span><span>
<div style="LINE-HEIGHT: 12pt"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 11pt; COLOR: black">Vision</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; COLOR: black">:&nbsp; </span><em><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; COLOR: black">A Lifelong Caring Home for Every Pet in KC</span></em></div>
<div style="LINE-HEIGHT: 12pt"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 11pt; COLOR: black">Mission</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; COLOR: black">:&nbsp;</span><em><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; COLOR: black"> To build a cooperative effort to transform the Greater Kansas City area into a No Kill community.</span></em></div>
<div style="LINE-HEIGHT: 12pt"><em><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; COLOR: black"></span></em></div>
<div style="LINE-HEIGHT: 12pt"><em><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; COLOR: black"></span></em></div>
<div style="LINE-HEIGHT: 12pt"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; COLOR: black">In support of our lifesaving efforts, No More Homeless Pets KC has launched a foster/adoption program to provide an alternative for cats and kittens&nbsp;to our community tradtional kill shelters.&nbsp; In 2008, we rescued more than 300 cats and kittens from area shelters and by the end of the year had placed 279 into loving homes.</span></div>
<div style="LINE-HEIGHT: 12pt"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; COLOR: black"></span></div>
<div style="LINE-HEIGHT: 12pt"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; COLOR: black"></span></div>
<div style="LINE-HEIGHT: 12pt"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; COLOR: black">No More Homeless Pets KC operates a high quality, high volume, low cost, spay/neuter program with the capacitiy to spay/neuter 16,000 pets per year.&nbsp; In addition, we operate the cities largest free roaming cat Trap/Neuter/Return program in the city and have an outreach program targeted to the inner city pets from disadvantaged neighborhoods.&nbsp; This Pet Uplift Program seeks out the backyard and chained dogs and works to improve their living conditions by providing dog houses, food and other resources.&nbsp; We help to educate the pet owners about better pet care and of the importance of having the pet spayed or neutered.</span></div>
