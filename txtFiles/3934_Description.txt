<p><font face="Comic Sans MS" size="3">TigeraCat Rescues, although not a&nbsp;&quot;shelter&quot;,&nbsp;provides&nbsp;cats and kittens&nbsp;(that would otherwise be euthanized by local humane societies &amp; animal control) with a 2nd chance through our &quot;no-kill&quot; philosophy.&nbsp; </font></p>
<p><font face="Comic Sans MS" size="3">TCR assists rescuers, owners, shelters, and others obtain a wide variety of resources.</font></p>
<ul>
    <li><font face="Comic Sans MS"><font size="3"><strong>Low-cost, quality vet care</strong> including, but not limited to:&nbsp;spay/neuter, vaccinations, microchips, parasite treatments, and more. </font></font></li>
    <li><font face="Comic Sans MS"><font size="3"><strong>Advertising &amp; Adoption services</strong>:&nbsp; Listing cats &amp; kittens from all walks of life up for adoption, screening potential adopters, and placing these precious animals in quality, forever homes. </font></font></li>
    <li><font face="Comic Sans MS"><font size="3"><strong>Trap-Neuter-Return</strong> <strong>programs &amp; education</strong>&nbsp;TCR strives to educate the public and other humane organizations of this viable alternative to euthanasia as a means of population and disease control when dealing with non-adoptable cats &amp; kittens of the feral population.&nbsp;&nbsp; </font></font></li>
    <li><font face="Comic Sans MS"><font size="3"><strong>Veterinary Referral</strong> </font></font></li>
    <li><font face="Comic Sans MS"><font size="3"><strong>Pet-Care assistance &amp; information.</strong> </font></font></li>
    <li><font face="Comic Sans MS"><font size="3"><strong>Literature,&nbsp;links, and educational materials</strong> in a wide-variety of feline-related fields of interest including basic pet care, disease management, humane treatment, abuse/neglect, and more!&nbsp;</font></font> </li>
</ul>
<p><font face="Comic Sans MS" size="3">We assist Iowa rescuers &amp; caregivers in maintaining feral-populations in a humane manner to relieve strain and free up the resources of existing humane societies &amp; animal control officials. </font></p>
<p align="center"><font face="Comic Sans MS"><font size="3"><strong>TigeraCat Rescues, Inc. is&nbsp;a non-profit, volunteer, &amp; donation operated organization for the humane treatment of cats and other domestic animals.</strong>&nbsp; </font></font></p>
<p align="center"><strong><font face="Comic Sans MS" size="4">There are no paid staff members, EVERYONE IS A VOLUNTEER!</font></strong></p>
