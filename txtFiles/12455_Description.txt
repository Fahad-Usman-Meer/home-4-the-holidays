<p>Second Chance - Mason Animal Rescue is a 501(c)3 that started in 2012. &nbsp;Mason is a small ranching community in central Texas whose local pound is a kill facility. &nbsp;Second Chance works on saving the dogs and cats brought in to the Mason pound by finding them foster or forever homes. &nbsp;Since 2012 Second Chance has rescued over 450 dogs and cats from this small community.</p>
