<p><strong style="line-height:1.6em">The Cat Corner Rescue Group Inc.&nbsp;</strong></p>

<p>The Cat Corner Rescue Group Inc is a genuine No-Kill, Not for Profit rescue &amp; re-homing service, that has been working with felines in need since 2007 &amp; became Knox City Council&rsquo;s first animal shelter in 2014.<br />
Our Mission is to be a leading Animal Welfare Charity &amp; to promote the No-Kill goal. We aspire to a world where No-Kill is known as an achievable goal, no healthy animal is destroyed &amp; every cat that comes into our care is adopted into a permanent loving home.</p>
