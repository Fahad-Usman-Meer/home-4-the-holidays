<p>Our mission: &nbsp;People-4-PAWS Animal Rescue&#39;s aim is to reduce the number of animals killed in our community by assisting animals in need through rescue for foster, &nbsp;adoption, and rehoming. &nbsp;Further People-4-PAWS mission is to provide education on the need to spay/neuter to reduce the population of throw away animals, work to provide low cost spay/neuter for our community and improve the laws to protect the animals of our&nbsp;community.</p>

<p>Our Goals:</p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p>It is the goal of People-4-PAWS Florida to:</p>

<p>1. Fundraise, through events, donations, sponsors and grants in order to-</p>

<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; a. provide funds for rescues to transport animals, board animals, provide medical care to animals, recruit foster homes and general operating needs in saving animals from shelters.</p>

<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; b. provide marketing and education to the public and lawmakers on the need to spay and neuter all pets (cats and dogs) to reduce the number of shelter animals and&nbsp;reduce the need for euthanizing adoptable cats and dogs.</p>

<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; c. meet additional needs of rescue of dogs and cats with purchase or lease of vans, gas, crates and leashes to establish a &nbsp;network throughout the state of Florida and eventually through the southeast for transporting animals to safety.</p>
