<p style="margin-bottom: 0.0001pt; line-height: normal;" class="MsoNormal"><span style="color: black;"><font color="#993366"><strong>Tavi &amp; Friends</strong> </font>is a 501c3 no-kill organization that helps senior, special needs and homeless animals in the NYC and LI areas, providing a safe haven until adopted or for life.<span style="">&nbsp; </span>Our key initiatives are:</span></p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0.0001pt; line-height: normal;" class="MsoNormal"><span style="color: black;">&bull; <strong><font color="#993366">Tavi&rsquo;s Curls Project</font>,</strong> rescues and re-homes American Curl cats<br />
</span><span style="color: black;">&bull; <font color="#993366"><strong>TTouch-in-Rescue program</strong></font>, providing Tellington TTouch training to shelter and rescue staff/volunteers to assist them in socializing animals, reducing behavior issues, enhancing wellness, increasing adoptables and reducing euthanasia numbers.<br />
&bull; <strong><font color="#993366">Foster Sanctuary program</font> </strong>provides a safe haven for all the animals we take in until they are adopted into their forever home.&nbsp; Many of the senior and special animals may not find their 'forever' home, but they have a loving home with us for life within our foster network<br />
&bull; <font color="#993366"><strong>TNR (Trap-Neuter-Return) program</strong></font>, a proven method to help reduce the over-population of feral and homeless cats.<span style="">&nbsp; </span><br />
</span><span style="color: black;">&bull; <font color="#993366"><strong>Rescue / Adoption.&nbsp; </strong></font></span><span style="color: black;">We rescue abandoned cats and kittens, and/or those who can be socialized, and adopt out into the right home.<br />
</span></p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0.0001pt; line-height: normal;" class="MsoNormal"><span style="color: black;">Please visit our website to see our animals for adoption and those needing sponsors.<span style="">&nbsp; </span>Daily and medical costs continue to escalate, and we need your support&nbsp; to continue in our mission to help these animals.</span><a>&nbsp; <font color="#003366"><strong>www.TAVIandFRIENDS.org</strong></font></a><span style="color: black;"></span></p>
