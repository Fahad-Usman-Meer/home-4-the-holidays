<h2>Mostly Mutts Animal Rescue &amp; Adoption, Inc.</h2>
<p><font size="3">Mostly Mutts conducts adoptions every Sat. at PetSmart on Barrett Pkwy., and Sun. at PetSmart in Acworth, on Cobb Pkwy. from noon until 4:30 pm.&nbsp; Our adoption fee is $175 and includes spay/neuter, mircochipping and all shots up to date.&nbsp; Any dog who comes into the program over 6 months old is tested for heartworm and if positive is treated at the expense of Mostly Mutts.&nbsp; To view our dogs visit our website at <a href="http://www.mostlymutts.org">www.mostlymutts.org</a> or <a href="http://www.petfinder.com">www.petfinder.com</a>&nbsp;&nbsp; Note: we do not adopt to anyone under 21 years of age.&nbsp; Please note we cannot take in strays or owner give ups.&nbsp; Our shelter is not that large and we <u>only</u> pull dogs from the county shelter.&nbsp;&nbsp;For every dog we would take from you one gets put down at animal control.&nbsp; You still have a chance of finding your dog a home.&nbsp; The dogs at animal control are on their last chance.</font></p>
