<p style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt" class="MsoBodyText"><strong><font color="#0000ff" size="3">SECOND CHANCE ANIMAL RESCUE<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>is a small,<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>entirely VOLUNTEER animal rescue group based in Lamar, Colorado. SECOND CHANCE rescues about 300 dogs and cats annually and saves hundreds more from euthanasia at the local shelter. Many of these dogs and cats have been abandoned or neglected. All of them just need a little TIME and LOVE, and that is what we give them.</font></strong></p>
