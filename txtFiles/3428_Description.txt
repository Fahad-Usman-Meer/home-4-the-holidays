<p>The Cat Network, Inc. (CN) is a 501(c)3, Florida not-for-profit corporation dedicated to humanely reducing cat overpopulation by educating the public about the need to sterilize their pets and strays; providing access to low-cost spay/neuter services for stray, homeless and abandoned cats; helping members in their efforts to place adoptable cats in loving homes; and advocating non-lethal population control and humane public policy. Join us at our <a target="_self" href="/get-involved-2/monthly-meetings/">monthly meetings</a> and learn more about how you can help homeless cats!</p>
