We are an Adoption Guarantee Facility.&nbsp; We have been chartered as a 501(c)(3) non profit organization since January 2007.&nbsp; We are also the Tennessee SPCA and answer all animal welfare concerns.&nbsp; We always try to educate the owners first, if possible, if not, then we take the animal(s) into our protective custody and proceed with prosecution according to Tennessee State Laws.&nbsp; We are proud to say (even though so unfortunate for the animals where they came from) &nbsp;we have rescued and helped over 2200 animals since being in operation.&nbsp; Animals of course, is our passion and compassion.&nbsp; We speak for those who cannot.
