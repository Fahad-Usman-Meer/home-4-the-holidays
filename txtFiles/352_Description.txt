<p align="center"><font color="#000000" size="2">The mission of the Anderson Humane Society is to make possible a better world for all animals by providing shelter, educating the community about humane care and treatment, advocating animal welfare, and promoting the human-animal bond. </font></p>
<p align="center"><font color="#000000" size="2">*The Humane Society was formed in 1981 to help solve the problem of unwanted dogs and cats in this region.</font></p>
<p align="center"><font color="#000000" size="2">*The Humane Society helps reunite lost pets with their loved ones.</font></p>
<p align="center"><font color="#000000" size="2">*Volunteer staff works with foster homes, rescue organizations, and trainers to place special-needs animals.</font></p>
<p align="center"><font color="#000000" size="2">*Humane Education Programs are carried out in public schools and organizations in Anderson County.</font></p>
<p align="center"><font color="#000000" size="2">*The Humane Society recognizes volunteers as the backbone of our organization, fundraising, and administrative operations.</font></p>
<p align="center"><font color="#000000" size="2">*The Humane Society is dedicated to putting a stop to animal abuse and neglect, serving in cruelty investigations and representing those who have no voice.</font></p>
<p align="center"><font color="#000000" size="2">*The Humane Society has a spay/neuter assistance program, in cooperation with local veterinarians (<strong><em>as of October 25, 2007, we have had 774 Anderson County animals fixed!</em></strong>).</font></p>
<p align="center"><font color="#000000" size="2">*The Society's newsletter is published once and month and is distributed to over 100 members.</font></p>
<p align="center"><font color="#000000" size="2">*The Humane Society puts on several community events including the annual Giant Rummage Sale, Pet Portraits, Calendar Photo Contest, our Howl-O-Ween Pet Costume Contest, monthly adoption events, and several other fundraisers.</font></p>
<p align="center"><font color="#000000" size="2">*Volunteers conduct offsite adoption days in local businesses to increase adoptions.</font></p>
<p align="center"><font color="#000000" size="2">The Humane Society is an all-volunteer organization. It has no paid employees or salaried officers. The Society receives no funding from local, state or federal governments. Operating expenses are supported by tax-deductible, voluntary contributions from the public. All money received is used solely for the animals. Please help us help the &quot;voiceless&quot;. To donate please send your contribution to:</font></p>
