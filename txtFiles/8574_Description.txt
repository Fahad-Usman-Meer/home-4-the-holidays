<p>We are a 501c3 non-profit rescue. We rely on donations provide food and vet care for the animals we rescue prior to adoption. We receive no money from the state or other governments. We are providing a Trap, Neuter, Release program for the stray feral cats in Hardee County to prevent unwanted kittens from being born on the streets homeless. Any and all donations are appreciated and used strictly for the care of the animals. We are a volunteer organization no paid employees.We also provide a Low cost Spay and Neuter service for dogs and cats.</p>
