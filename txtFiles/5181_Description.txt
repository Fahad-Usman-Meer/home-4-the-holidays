<p>We are Paws 2 Your Heart Animal Rescue, a No-kill rescue that runs only on donations &amp; various fundraising events. We don't recieve any state funding. Our purpose is to pull animals from 'High-Kill' facilities and give them a second chance at life. <br />
Our Mission: <br />
* To pull dogs &amp; cats from other shelters that are about to be put to sleep.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; * To Rehabilitate Abused and homeless animals<br />
* To Prevent Cruelty to Animals. <br />
* To provide shelter and medical care to homeless animals. <br />
* To educate the public about responsible pet care &amp; ownership. </p>
