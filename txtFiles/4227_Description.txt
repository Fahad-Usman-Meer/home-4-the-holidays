Randolph County Humane Society is a 501 c 3 not for Profit shelter, limited space, NO KILL shelter, we are a very hard working group of people that keep our doors open by donations, and fundraising. We get NO State, County, City or Federal help, the doors stay open to continue our mission to save animals that come into animal controls in Randolph County where they only have limited time.
WE RESCUE from abuse situations, and give them another chance for a happy and healthy life. Any and all donations keep our mission to save lives one by one happing everyday. True dedication and love for all of the animals is something that we show every day.

We are located behind the Lion's Club in Sparta, IL.
