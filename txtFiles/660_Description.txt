<p>SICSA is a non-profit, limited intake adoption facility. SICSA provides cat and dog adoptions and is focused on finding the perfect forever homes for their animals. SICSA also provides low cost spay and neuter, educational programs, birthday parties, and pet therapy. SICSA has several volunteer opportunities avialable. </p>
<p>Visit SICSA online at <a href="http://www.sicsa.org">www.sicsa.org</a>, Facebook and YouTube. SICSA is open seven days a week Monday - Saturday 12-7 and Sunday 12-4. Stop by and visit with SICSA's wonderful animals and maybe find your forever friend. </p>
