Broken Arrow Animal Sanctuary is a no kill shelter.&nbsp; At the moment we have kittens, cats, rabbits, guinea pig to be adopted.&nbsp; Our shelter has approximatley 150 pets to be adopted;&nbsp; We test the animals for disease, spay/neuter and give vaccinations before the animal is adopted.&nbsp; We deliver the pet to the home to ensure a good relationship with a family and possible pets already at the home.
