<p>Cats Haven&rsquo;s Mission:</p>

<p>To provide a non-institutional shelter (no cages) for cats and kittens. Cats Haven was founded for special needs cats or kittens with birth defects, visual impairments, neurological problems, or medical issues or injuries that require veterinary care.&nbsp; Cats Haven strives to educate the public about available resources and proper care of companion animals.&nbsp;</p>
