<span class="style16">Whisker Rescue - Enriching lives, one cat at a time.<br />
</span><br />
<p class="style12"><span class="style17">Whisker Rescue is a non-profit charitable organization (#85142 3426 RR0001) located in Red Deer, Alberta, that strives to provide basic needs such as shelter, food, medical attention, and adoption opportunities to </span><a class="style15" href="http://whiskerrescue.com/cats.html">stray, homeless &amp; surrendered cats</a> <span class="style18">in our care.&nbsp; We are a no-kill organization that relies on volunteers to give every cat that enters our adoption program the time, patience, love and cuddles they need until their forever homes can be found.</span>&nbsp;</p>
<p class="style29">Each cat is vet checked, tested for Feline Leukemia and FIV viruses, vaccinated, de-wormed, spayed or neutered, and tattooed or micro-chipped. The adoption fee is $125, and cats 8 years and older are $65.</p>
<p class="style12"><span class="style20">We are fortunate to work in cooperation with several facilities in Red Deer that have generously donated space to help us adopt our lucky felines into loving homes.&nbsp;</span> </p>
<p class="style12"><span class="style24">Our cats can be visited and adopted through the</span> <a target="_blank" href="http://www.deerparkpet.ca/">Deer Park Pet Hospital </a><span class="style23">at 420-Allan Street, </span><a target="_blank" href="http://www.pipercreekveterinary.ca/">Piper Creek Veterinary Hospital</a> <span class="style21">at #1-166-37428 Range Road 273 or our Whisker Rescue Adoption room located in the </span><span class="style26">All In One Pet Care Facility</span><span class="style21"> at 4831-53rd Street, and at </span><a target="_blank" href="http://www.petsmart.com/">PetSmart </a><span class="style22">located in RedDeer at 5001-19th Street, and in Edmonton at 3289 Calgary Trail. </span></p>
