<p>Friends of Rescue (also known as Friends of Retrievers Rescue) rescue all breeds of dogs and cats from local shelters and adopt to the Huntsville Area but also adopt to New England states as well as Tennesee.&nbsp; </p>
<p>We are dedicated to finding responsible homes for our fosters and educating the public on spay/neuter and rescue.&nbsp; </p>
<p>Last year we rescued around 700 dogs and cats.&nbsp; </p>
