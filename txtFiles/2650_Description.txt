Feral Friends of AZ is a charitable 501c3 organization.&nbsp; Our primary mission is the sterlization of feral, primarlily free roaming, cats on school campuses.&nbsp; As a byproduc of these efforts, we have an adoption program to adopt out tame cats and young kittens that are soclialized.&nbsp; Adoption fees include: sterilization, Rabies vaccination, upper respitory combo vaccines until adopted, food/water bowl, bag of cat toys, and an adoption kit to help get your new family member started off on the right paw in a new home.&nbsp; Adoption fees are $50 for every available cat and kitten during our Home 4 the Holidays campaign!
