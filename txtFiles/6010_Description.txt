<p><font face="Arial">Shar Pei Rescue is a small group of self funded volunteers in Sydney, Adelaide, Melbourne and Brisbane who save Shar Pei from pounds and shelters around Australia.</font></p>
<font face="Arial">
<p><br />
We desex, microchip, vaccinate, worm, flea treat and heartworm test all dogs including puppies.</p>
<p><br />
Shar Pei Rescue was started in 2004 to help Shar Pei that were no longer wanted.</p>
<p><br />
We only rehome dogs of good temperament into homes experienced in dominant breeds that are able to keep dogs for life.</p>
<p><br />
Please visit us at <a href="http://www.sharpeirescue.com.au">www.sharpeirescue.com.au</a></p>
<p><br />
Shar Pei Rescue is not for profit any donations go towards helping the dogs and would be greatly appreciated. </p>
