<p>Spenser's Legacy Animal Rescue is a 501(c)(3) no-kill, non-profit, all-volunteer organization dedicated to the rescue of lost, abandoned, sick, abused, feral,&nbsp;and stray animals (domestic, farm, or wild) in the Fayetteville, Arkansas area. It was started in the summer of 2006 and now includes approximately 35 cats and kittens, 10 dogs, disabled bunnies, chickens, and a senior horse.</p>
<p>We want to find permanent,loving homes for the animals we rescue. Any animal that might be unadoptable will always have a lifetime home here at Spenser's Legacy Animal Rescue.</p>
