Rohe Animal Rescue (RAR) is a not for profit, no-kill, small, private run organization. We at Rohe Animal Rescue rescue, rehabilitate, spay/neuter, foster and adopt out adoptable animals. RAR is also a sanctuary to those animals that need to stay with us to live out their lives happily in a loving family environment. We receive no federal or state funding and are run strictly by volunteers. We are not a shelter,all of our rescues are housed within foster homes only and we work by appointments only. Our foster homes are located in the Raleigh, Durham NC &amp; surrounding areas.
